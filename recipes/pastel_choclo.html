PASTEL DE CHOCLO

Traditional dish fromChile, typically made with a filling of pino (ground beef mixed with onions). A piece of chicken, raisins, olives and hard-boiled egg are also commonly added and topped with a layer of sweet corn purée. The sweet corn topping is usually mixed with basil or other herbs to add flavor.

INGREDIENTS

Corn Layer

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 bags of corn (460g)
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 3 tablespoon of cormeal
  • 10 leaves of basil
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Meat Layer

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 cup of water or beef broth
  • 3 onios. Diced small
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of merkén
  • Salt and pepper

Optional

  • 1 piece of cooked chicken
  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • 2-3 black olives
  • 2 raisins
